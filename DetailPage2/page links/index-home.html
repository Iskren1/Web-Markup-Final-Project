Nowadays a lot of people immigrate in Netherlands in order to study for an Academic degree. The reasons why the majority of people chose to study in Netherlands are quite a few. One of those is that the Dutch educational institutions offer a high quality tuition and the price is considerably lower than in many English speaking countries. Our goal is to give a better idea to the international students on how to immigrate comfortably and get around easily on arrival.
