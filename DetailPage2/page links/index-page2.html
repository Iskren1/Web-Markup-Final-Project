It is really important to have some idea of how to settle in and get comfortable before moving out to Netherlands. Below there are some advises on how to do so:

STUDENT HOUSING It is really hard to find housing in Netherlands, especially for international students. There are also scammers, so be aware of that before making a decision with a housing. Students can also find accommodations. They can stay in various types of accommodation. They can choose to live in a student house or flat, which is a common thing to do for Dutch students. Some organizations that can help with housings are Clawq, DUWO, Nestpick, Plaza residence services, etc.

2. INTRODUCTION WEEK Typically, Universities organize an introduction week for international students. There are some fun activities which is a great opportunity for students to make friends and get familiar with the University itself

UPON ARRIVAL:

  • Register at your municipality to get your BSN
  • Open a bank account
  • Obtain a DigiD
  • Take out Dutch Health Insurance
  • Get a Dutch sim card
