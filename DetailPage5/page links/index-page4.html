The importance of the factors that needs to be taken into consideration before moving out to Netherlands must not be ignored.

BEFORE YOU MOVE

  • Research different programmes that apply to your study choice
  • Check your admissions offer
  • Arrange your visa
  • Arrange your residence permit
  • Arrange (long tem) accommodation
  • Arrange transport to the Netherlands
  • Find out whether your ID or passport card is still valid
  • Prepare documents for the Netherlands
    • birth certificates
    • diplomas and qualifications of previous education
    • medical records
    • proof of health insurance
  • Make an appointment to register with the municipalit
